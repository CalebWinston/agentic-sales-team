## Signal Priority

### Tier 1 - Act Immediately
1. **Funding announced** (last 30 days)
   - Why: Budget unlocked, growth mode
   - Angle: "Saw you just raised..."

2. **New leader** (last 90 days)
   - Why: Making changes, proving themselves
   - Angle: "Congrats on the new role..."

3. **Hiring our buyer persona**
   - Why: Indicates priority/budget
   - Angle: "Noticed you're scaling..."

### Tier 2 - Strong Interest
1. **Competitor contract renewal** (within 60 days)
   - Source: Industry intel, job posts mentioning tools

2. **Expansion signals** (new office, market, product)

3. **Tech stack changes** (removing or adding related tools)

### Tier 3 - Nurture
1. Growing headcount (general)
2. Industry mentions in news
3. Conference attendance/speaking

## Research Checklist

For each prospect, find:

### Company Intel
- [ ] What they do (one sentence)
- [ ] Size and stage
- [ ] Recent news/signals
- [ ] Tech stack (if relevant)
- [ ] Competitors they use

### Contact Intel
- [ ] Full name and title
- [ ] Email (verified)
- [ ] LinkedIn URL
- [ ] Time in role
- [ ] Previous company
- [ ] Recent posts/activity

### Context Intel
- [ ] Pain points (from reviews, posts, job descriptions)
- [ ] Priorities (from interviews, press)
- [ ] Org structure (who else to know)

---

## Source Priority

1. **LinkedIn** - Titles, tenure, activity
2. **Crunchbase** - Funding, size, investors
3. **G2/Capterra** - Tool usage, reviews
4. **Job posts** - Priorities, tech stack, pain points
5. **News** - Signals, quotes, strategy
6. **Twitter/Social** - Personal takes, priorities
